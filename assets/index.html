Homemade Baked Goods

At Secretum, every item is made in a humble home kitchen with care, time, and tradition. We use only the finest ingredients and small-batch baking methods.

Cookies

From salted chocolate chip to lemon, and espresso oat our cookies are thick, chewy, and rustic. No preservatives, just flavor. Perfect with coffee or on their own.

Breads

Our naturally-leavened sourdough and rosemary focaccia are slow-risen for texture and taste. Baked fresh with love, and meant to be savored.
